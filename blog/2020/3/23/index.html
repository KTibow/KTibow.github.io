    Okay. You've learned about how bad Facebook is. Now what? Protect yourself.

    There are multiple ways to do this. I'll go over them.

    The most protective:

  • Request for Facebook to delete all of your data, including posts. Make sure you've downloaded it first.
  • Uninstall the Facebook App, and your Facebook Account.
  • Don't use Facebook again, and share with your friends using the buttons at the bottom of the page.

    But of course, you still want to use Facebook. Then do these:

  • Request for Facebook to delete all of your tracking data.
  • Remove all of your photos. Use Firefox Send to share your photos as a Facebook link. Set up a password, and share it with your friends mouth-to-mouth.
  • Use Firefox on desktop and Firefox on mobile, and install Facebook Container to stop Facebook from doing internet-wide tracking.
  • If a video is unoriginal, report it. Facebook counts views as 3 seconds of autoplay, so if you scroll at a slow pace then this is really important.
  • Delete the Facebook app. It's just plain terrible. If you still need Messenger, download Messenger Lite. It doesn't have video calls, but for all that you know they could start turning that into training data, because their Privacy Policy and Terms Of Service could change anytime. If you still need Facebook on your phone, use m.facebook.com instead.
